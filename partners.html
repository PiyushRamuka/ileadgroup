Change Pro
ChangePro is a unique simulation to help leaders implements and manage the process of change. Change Pro is used by global leading organizations and top business schools to implement change initiatives. A computer based simulation tool aimed to aid managers to enhance and develop their ability to establish and implement change effectively, Change Pro is highly recommended for managers to deal with change through awareness, interest, evaluation and adoption. Change Pro is the tool for an organizations growth and success, as it is vital for the change agents to manage evolution as seamlessly as possible. ChangePro is based on studies by Professor Jean- Francois Manzoni… Shell chaired professor of Human Resources & Organizational Development and Professor of Management Practice at INSEAD. This is tool is highly recommended specially if you want to skill your leaders and Managers to implement new changes in your organization. This tool is used extensively across many organizations and is also part of the leadership curriculum in top business schools – INSEAD & MIT among others.

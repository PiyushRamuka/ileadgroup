About iLead
iLead focuses on developing "Leadership" talent. Our endeavor is to create a better environment for future generations through enlightened leadership.

We work with organizations and individuals to ignite and evolve their leadership talent. Helping them develop appropriate skills and capability. We believe in developing “Authentic Leaders” who lead with their head and heart to create an inspirational environment. Such leaders inspire others through transformation in self and influence people and organizations to achieve greatness.

iLead partners with you to provide customized solutions and also offers its own Signature programs. Our solutions are experiential, leveraging a global pool of associate network and our strategic knowledge partnerships. Our facilitators and Coaches have international experience with deep industry knowledge.

We Value and promise you our Trust, Integrity, Passion and Commitment.

iLead Inception
Maneesha Kapur is the founder and driving force behind iLead Learning & Development. A Facilitator and Coach, her journey is to help people transform, learn new ways of thinking and doing, challenge status quo, supersede their own expectations from themselves. Her inner desire and hope is to leave the world with leaders who lead with their heart and head. Somewhere, somehow..... leave a better world for our future generations!

With more than two decades of extensive experience in adult learning, serving many multinational companies and global businesses, her passion is to bring change and help organizations and individuals grow.

At iLead, Maneesha is dedicating her Knowledge, Skills and Intuition to help people 'find the strength to grow', to help organizations develop and people to adapt, transform, ignite, enhance and challenge the leader within.
